Welcome to The Top Ten (the "Blog"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while you visit our Blog. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use, disclose, and safeguard that information.

Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal identification information about your interactions with the Blog. This may include your browser name, the type of device used, and technical information about your means of connection to our Blog, such as the operating system and the Internet service providers utilized.

Our Blog may use "cookies" to enhance user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand how users interact with our Blog. You can choose to set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent.

We may use third-party services like Google Analytics to monitor and analyze web traffic. These services may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, including cookies and your IP address.

Data Security

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information stored on our Blog.

Our Blog includes a contact form powered by [Formspree](https://formspree.io/). When you submit a message through the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiries. Form submissions are processed by Formspree, and their [Privacy Policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy) applies to the handling of your data.

Children's Privacy

Our Blog does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.
